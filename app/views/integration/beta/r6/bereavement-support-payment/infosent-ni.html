What happens next

  • You should get a decision within 15 working days. We’ll contact you if we need more information.
  • If your application is successful, we’ll make a payment into your account as soon as possible.

If you have any questions, call the Bereavement Support Payment team on 0800 085 2463 (option 2).

Help with funeral costs
You could be eligible for help with funeral costs if you get certain benefits. Check if you can get help to pay for the funeral.

Help with living costs and rates

You might be eligible for benefits or help with rates.

To check, you can:

  • use the 'Make the call' service by calling for free on 0800 232 1271
  • contact the Land and Property Services on 0300 200 7802 to see if you can get help with rates
  • use a benefits calculator
  • visit your local Jobs and Benefits office

Local advice agencies can also give you support and guidance.

Emotional support

Cruse Bereavement Care is a voluntary organisation you can contact for emotional help and support during a bereavement.

You can call them for free on 0808 808 1677.

You can also find other organisations who offer bereavement support, help and information.
